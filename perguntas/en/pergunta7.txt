📌 Pergunta:

What is the purpose of the "Messages and Alerts Overview" in SAP Business One?

🔘 Alternatives:

A) Send text messages (SMS) to anyone's personal cell phone.

B) Notify users about important events, such as low stock or pending approvals. ✅

C) Post photos and videos of company parties.

D) Block the computer of employees who arrive late.

E) Measure server temperature in real-time.

F) Play background music on company premises.

✅ Correct response: B

✔ Justification: B
The alert system allows the ERP to be proactive. You can set it up to receive alerts when a customer exceeds their credit limit or when an item reaches the minimum stock level, allowing for quick action.

❌ SE O USUÁRIO ERRAR:

👉 Se responder A:
❌ Incorrect
SMS alerts require external integration services; it is not the standard primary function.

👉 Se responder C:
❌ Incorrect
SAP B1 is a management tool, not a corporate social network.

👉 Se responder D:
❌ Incorrect
Access is controlled by login, but there is no native "late block".

👉 Se responder E:
❌ Incorrect
This is the function of infrastructure (IT) monitoring software.

👉 Se responder F:
❌ Incorrect
SAP B1 does not have an integrated audio player for background music.
