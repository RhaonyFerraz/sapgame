📌 Pergunta:

What does a "Business Partner" represent in the context of SAP Business One?

🔘 Alternatives:

A) Only the company's internal employees.

B) Only the banks where the company has an account.

C) Customers, Vendors, and Leads (Potential Customers). ✅

D) Only the third-party software installed on the server.

E) Anyone who visits the company's website, even without interacting.

F) Exclusively the organization's managing partners.

✅ Correct response: C

✔ Justification: C
The Business Partner (BP) master data is unified. The same partner can be a customer and a vendor (although with distinct linked registrations). The "Lead" type is used in the CRM module for prospects who haven't closed a sale yet.

❌ SE O USUÁRIO ERRAR:

👉 Se responder A:
❌ Incorrect
Employees have a specific registration in the HR module.

👉 Se responder B:
❌ Incorrect
Banks are configured in "House Banks", although they can exist as Business Partners if you buy services from them.

👉 Se responder D:
❌ Incorrect
This would be assets or extensions, not commercial partners.

👉 Se responder E:
❌ Incorrect
Anonymous visitors are not listed in the ERP until there is a record of interest/lead.

👉 Se responder F:
❌ Incorrect
Partners are registered in corporate data or as users, as needed.
