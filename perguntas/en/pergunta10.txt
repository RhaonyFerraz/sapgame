📌 Pergunta:

How does SAP Business One help in managing "Batches and Serial Numbers"?

🔘 Alternatives:

A) It generates random numbers just for label decoration.

B) It allows tracking each unit or group of products from entry up to the sale to the end customer. ✅

C) It renames all products as "Batch 1" to simplify.

D) It deletes purchase history every 30 days to save space.

E) It forces the user to memorize all serial numbers by heart.

F) It works only with products that do not have barcodes.

✅ Correct response: B

✔ Justification: B
Traceability is crucial for food, medicine, and electronics industries. With SAP B1, you know exactly which supplier delivered a specific batch and to which customer it was sold, facilitating Recall processes.
