📌 Pergunta:

In SAP Business One, what is the main difference between Exchange Rates and Indices?

🔘 Alternatives:

A) Exchange rates are used to convert values between currencies, while indices correct or update values over time. ✅

B) Exchange rates update rental contracts, while indices convert dollar or euro values to local currency.

C) Exchange rates are used only in reports, while indices record international purchases and sales.

D) Exchange rates define taxes on transactions, while indices control customer accounts.

E) Exchange rates correct values over time, while indices convert foreign currencies to local currency.

F) Exchange rates and indices have the same function, serving only to record accounting entries.

✅ Correct response: A

✔ Justification: A
Alternative A is correct because it exactly reflects the function of each resource in SAP B1:

Exchange rates → converting foreign currency values to local currency.
Indices → correcting or updating financial values over time, such as contract adjustments or inflation updates.

❌ SE O USUÁRIO ERRAR (responder B, C, D, E ou F):

👉 Se responder B:
❌ Incorrect
Exchange rates do not update contracts; indices are not used for currency conversion. Both functions are inverted.

👉 Se responder C:
❌ Incorrect
Exchange rates are not just for reports, and indices do not record international purchases and sales directly.

👉 Se responder D:
❌ Incorrect
Exchange rates do not define taxes, and indices do not control customer accounts directly.

👉 Se responder E:
❌ Incorrect
Exchange rates do not correct values over time; that is the function of indices. And indices do not convert currencies.

👉 Se responder F:
❌ Incorrect
Exchange rates and indices do not have the same function; each has a specific purpose in the system.

🎁 BÔNUS:
“Exchange Rates and Indices” is used to control foreign currency values and economic indices that impact finance and accounting.

💱 Exchange Rates
These are the conversion values between currencies.

📌 What are they for?
• Converting foreign currency values → local currency
• Recording:
  - International purchases
  - Sales in dollar/euro
  - Payments and receipts

📊 Indices
Used for corrections and updates of values, usually linked to inflation or contracts.

📌 What are they for?
• Readjusting contracts (rent, services, etc.)
• Updating financial values based on economic indices
